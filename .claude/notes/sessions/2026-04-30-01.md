### 5. `/kip/` PNG corruption fix (critical)
All three `/customerconnect-ai/public/kip/` files (`hero.png`, `portrait.png`, `orb.png`) were binary-corrupt: `0x89` bytes mangled to `ef bf bd` (UTF-8 replacement character U+FFFD) throughout the entire file body — result of a prior text-mode write.

Files were unrecoverable. Regenerated from clean source using PIL:
- `hero.png/webp` ← `app-landing-page-primary-kip.png` (339×418 RGBA, transparency preserved)
- `portrait.png/webp` ← `kip__2d-icon-ui-asset-system__2d-bust-crop.png` → 512×512 on dark bg `(18,18,28)`
- `orb.png/webp` ← `hero-floating-head-orb-kip.png` → 256×256 on dark bg `(18,18,28)`
