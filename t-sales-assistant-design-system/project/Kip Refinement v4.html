01 / The Character Itself

Locked. Non-negotiable. The source of truth for every implementation.

Silhouette

T-Mobile utility jacket · glossy black helmet with magenta visor · confident forward stance. Reads "operator," not "mascot." Works at any size: 16px pinned orb to full-screen hero.

The 8 Expressions

Happy · Focused · Thinking · Helpful · Excited · Determined · Surprised · Playful. Eye-as-expression. Locked shapes. Licensable. Memorable at 32px and 320px.

Sidekick Core Device

The transparent, glowing handheld. Shows up beside Kip in hero moments. T-colored rim, magenta data streams, holographic glow. Reinforces "knowledge strengthens the signal."

Rendering

The poster-quality full-body character is the gold standard. All implementations (CSS visor, low-res orbit icons, runner figures, mobile takeovers) derive from this one source. Kip's proportions, jacket folds, and visor shine are baked into every downstream version.

Five sizes. One character. Same voice, same energy, always recognizable.

◎

32–40px. Appears inline with copy: one-line nudge, auto-dismisses in 6s. Never intrudes.

◉

◯

Full companion

Full-figure, 320+px. Docked rail during live call, sidebar during training. Persistent.

⊙
05 · Takeover

Hero moment

Full-screen. Onboarding, post-close celebration, daily morale kickoff. Kip alone with you.

All sizes share the same expression, proportions, and voice. Kip is always Kip.
Core rule

One character

Never two Kips on screen. Never two recommendations. Kip's presence is always earned, always singular, always clear.

03 / Where Kip Lives

Six surfaces from the brief. Same Kip. Different contexts. Different moments. Same unstoppable energy.

Sales Assistant

Live call. Kip in the right-rail panel. Listening, flagging objections, ready with the next move. Eyes track the transcript in real-time. Sidebar companion shows the rep's daily stats — Kip is a teammate, not a tool.

Tech Guide

Diagnostic. Kip walks the rep through fixes step-by-step. Determined expression. Clear, technical voice. "Try a tower reset before swap. 3 mins." Always give the exact phrase to say.

Teacher

Training mode. Kip breaks plans down into bite-sized wins. Helpful expression. Gamified. Persistent panel throughout the lesson. Streaks, XP, celebration animations on nailed scenarios.

Home Internet Savant

Coverage setup. Kip annotates coverage maps and router placement. Thinking expression while optimizing. Inline whispers next to the map: "Mesh suggested for 3-room." Confident and specific.

Travel Companion

Roaming awareness. Kip knows which countries are covered, data limits, and country-specific tips. Happy expression. "Canada coverage: talk + text + 5GB data — no add-on needed." Safe to confirm, safe to say.

Gaming Coach

Runner mini-game. Full-body Kip, full-screen takeover. Excited expression, star eyes, celebration when you hit streaks. "Win streak · +320 XP." The character is the game.

04 / The Voice Never Changes

Confident. Curious. Empathetic. Playful. Reliable. Rules over every surface, every context.

Always

• Lead with the action, never the explanation
• One recommendation per surface
• Give the rep an exact phrase they can say
• Celebrate wins loudly, fix mistakes quietly
• Never interrupt during customer speech

Never

• "Great question!" / "I'd be happy to help"
• Longer than two sentences in Whisper
• Animate during customer talking (except listening)
• Appear without a reason
• Use first-person plural — Kip speaks as himself
